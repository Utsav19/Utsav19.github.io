Utsav Sadana
Email: utsav.sadana-at-hec.ca

I am a PhD student in Management Science at HEC Montréal, and a researcher at GERAD. I am advised by Prof. Georges Zaccour. I work primarily with Prof. Zaccour and Prof. Puduru Vishwanadha Reddy in the field of dynamic games. I am also currently working on a project with Prof. Erick Delage on Distributionally Robust Optimization.

Earlier, I graduated from IIT Kanpur with Bachelors in Materials Science and Engineering, and Masters in Economics. In my Master's thesis, I worked with Dr. Debayan Pakrashi on the estimation of corruption in the judiciary of Bangladesh.
