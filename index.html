Utsav Sadana
Email: utsav.sadana-at-hec.ca

I am a Postdoctoral fellow in the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University working with Prof. Mehmet Gumus, Prof. Erick Delage, and Prof. Angelos Georghiou. I received my Ph.D. in Management Science from HEC Montréal advised by Prof. Georges Zaccour. Before that, I graduated from IIT Kanpur with Bachelors in Materials Science and Engineering, and Masters in Economics. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to work closely with Prof. Zaccour, Prof. Puduru Viswanadha Reddy, and Prof. Delage. From September 2019 to February 2020, I was a visiting research scholar at the Coordinated Science Laboratory of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I worked with Prof. Tamer Başar.

My research interests lie in differential game theory, impulse optimal control theory, robust optimization, and their applications in environmental regulation, security and pricing problems.
