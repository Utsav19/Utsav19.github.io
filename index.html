Utsav Sadana

Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University with Prof. Mehmet Gumus, Prof. Erick Delage, and Prof. Angelos Georghiou.

I received my Ph.D. in Management Science from HEC Montréal advised by Prof. Georges Zaccour. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to also collaborate with Prof. Puduru Viswanadha Reddy, Prof. Delage and Prof. Tamer Başar.

Before that, I graduated from IIT Kanpur with Bachelors in Materials Science and Engineering, and Masters in Economics.

My current research interests lie in understanding the value of decision-focused learning (a.k.a end-to-end learning that combines optimization and machine learning) over the classical predict-then-optimize models, particularly in newsvendor problems. My research spans the fields of differential game theory, impulse control, robust optimization, deep learning, and their applications in environmental regulation, security, and retail.
