Utsav Sadana

I am a PhD candidate in Management Science advised by Prof. Georges Zaccour at HEC Montréal, and a researcher at GERAD. I am fortunate to work closely with Prof. Zaccour, Prof. Puduru Viswanadha Reddy, and Prof. Erick Delage.

From September 2019 to February 2020, I was a visiting research scholar at the Coordinated Science Laboratory of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I worked with Prof. Tamer Başar.

Earlier, I graduated from IIT Kanpur with Bachelors in Materials Science and Engineering, and Masters in Economics. In my Master's thesis, I worked with Dr. Debayan Pakrashi on the estimation of corruption in the judiciary of Bangladesh.
