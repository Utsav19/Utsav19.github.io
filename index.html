Utsav Sadana

Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University with Prof. Mehmet Gumus and Prof. Erick Delage.

I received my Ph.D. in Management Science from HEC Montréal advised by Prof. Georges Zaccour. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to also collaborate with Prof. Puduru Viswanadha Reddy, Prof. Delage and Prof. Tamer Başar.

Before that, I graduated from IIT Kanpur with Bachelors in Materials Science and Engineering, and Masters in Economics.

My research interests lie in differential game theory, impulse optimal control theory, robust optimization, and their applications in environmental regulation, security, and pricing problems.
